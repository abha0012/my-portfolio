About me

Hi, I'm Abha Gawande, a business analyst driven by data and inspired by impact. With a foundation in analytics and hands-on experience at a fast-paced startup, I specialize in transforming raw data into actionable strategies that improve product performance, enhance user experience, and drive revenue growth.

My journey into data began with curiosity wanting to understand not just what happened, but why. Working at the intersection of business and product teams, I’ve led A/B tests, built customer cohorts, and developed data-driven logic for subscription retention and discount optimization. These experiences helped me realize the power of data in shaping real business decisions.

What truly excites me is solving problems at scale using statistics, experimentation, and machine learning to answer strategic questions. That’s why I’m actively upskilling to become a data scientist. I’m diving deeper into supervised learning, predictive modeling, and building end-to-end projects that go beyond dashboards tools that not only inform but also act.

Outside of work, I love exploring real-world case studies, mentoring peers, and writing about data topics in a way that’s approachable and practical. I believe in continuous learning, and I’m proof that you don’t need a traditional data science degree to make meaningful contributions—you need curiosity, consistency, and a clear goal.

Let’s connect whether it’s about analytics, career transitions, data science, or creating meaningful change with data.
